October 28, 1992

The Professional and Amateur Sports Protection Act (PASPA) was signed into law by President George H. W. Bush effectively making sports betting illegal in the United States. The law excluded Nevada. In addition, sports lotteries in Montana, Oregon and Delaware were exempt from the laws restricitions.
