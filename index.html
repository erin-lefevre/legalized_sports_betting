The Adoption of Legalized Sports Betting across the United States.

Text, Text, Text - Betting on sport games is a controversial subject around the world and collectively represents a 150 billion dollar industry. In the United States, the industry has been growing and changing since the 1970's as as more and more states adopt new laws that allow betting on sports teams either in-person or online through regulated channels.
